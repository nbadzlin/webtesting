History

Early childhood

Elsa was born on the winter solstice[5] to King Agnarr and Queen Iduna of Arendelle. She weighed seven pounds, and was born during the occurrence of the aurora borealis. Her birth was also accompanied by an unanticipated weather event that blanketed the village with crystals of ice.
